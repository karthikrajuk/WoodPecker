IT Solutions.

IT Solutions for Your Business Needs

At Woodpecker Expert Solutions, we offer comprehensive IT solutions tailored to meet the diverse needs of your business. With a team of professionals possessing deep expertise in both hardware and software, we are dedicated to providing end-to-end solutions designed to optimise your operations and enhance productivity.

At Woodpecker Expert Solutions, we pride ourselves on delivering innovative and reliable IT solutions that empower your business to thrive in today’s fast-paced digital world. From hardware to software, MDM to tech enablement, we provide everything you need to boost efficiency, secure your data, and facilitate seamless collaboration across your teams. Let us be your trusted partner in IT, providing expert solutions that align with your business objectives.

End-Point Devices

We specialise in providing cutting-edge Apple devices including Macintosh, iPads, iPhones, and all related accessories to help your team stay productive and connected. Whether you’re looking for the latest models or need expert guidance on deployment, we’ve got you covered.

Cloud Storage & Collaboration

Our cloud solutions enable seamless storage, sharing, and collaboration across teams, ensuring that your business stays connected and efficient, no matter where you are.

Boardroom & Meeting Room Solutions

We deliver state-of-the-art audiovisual solutions for your boardrooms and meeting rooms, including high-quality audio systems, video conferencing setups, screens, projectors, wireless presenters, and meeting room schedulers. We help you create an environment that fosters collaboration and enhances communication.

AI-Driven HR Software Solutions

Our AI-powered software solutions optimise human resources management, from recruitment to performance tracking, enabling smarter, more efficient decision-making.

Storage & Backup Solutions

Secure your business data with our reliable storage and backup solutions. We provide scalable options to meet your organisation’s needs, ensuring that critical information is always protected.

Third-Party Hardware Accessories

We offer a wide variety of third-party accessories, including adapters, docking stations, and peripherals, ensuring that your devices are equipped to handle any task.

Customised Tech Bags & Accessories

Our bespoke tech bags, tablet cases, and laptop sleeves are designed for both style and functionality, providing the perfect protection and organisation for your devices on the go.

MDM (Mobile Device Management)

We offer advanced MDM solutions to help you securely manage and monitor all your devices from a central platform. Whether you’re deploying Apple devices or other mobile tech, our MDM services ensure seamless integration, data protection, and compliance with company policies.

Tech Enablement Jumpstarts

Our Tech Enablement Jumpstarts provide rapid deployment solutions designed to get your teams up and running with the latest technology quickly and efficiently. From onboarding devices to configuring software and setting up collaboration tools, we offer tailored solutions that ensure your business is ready to leverage technology effectively from day one.
